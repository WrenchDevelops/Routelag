# Zer0 Acceptable Use Policy

**Document version:** `2026-07-18.1`  
**Effective date:** `2026-07-18`  
**Operator:** WrenchDevelops

## Permitted use

- Personal testing of Zer0 game-routing for Fortnite (and other games only if explicitly authorized by Zer0)  
- Using the optional free Overwolf HUD as provided  
- Sharing feedback with the Zer0 team through approved channels  
- Exporting diagnostics for support when requested  

## Prohibited use

You may **not**:

1. Use Zer0 to attack, scan, spam, or abuse networks or third parties  
2. Attempt to inspect, intercept, modify, or sell **other users’ traffic content**  
3. Resell, sublicense, or publicly redistribute private-beta builds or invite codes  
4. Bypass authentication, entitlement, device limits, or node capacity controls  
5. Use Zer0 for unlawful surveillance, fraud, or copyright infringement  
6. Interfere with anti-cheat, inject into Fortnite, or modify game files via Zer0 (Zer0 is not designed to do this; do not attempt to make it do so)  
7. Use Zer0 to harass players or automate gameplay in violation of Epic rules  
8. Mine cryptocurrency or run unrelated high-bandwidth workloads through Zer0 nodes  
9. Probe Zer0 infrastructure for vulnerabilities outside a coordinated disclosure process approved by the Zer0 team  
10. Misrepresent affiliation with Epic Games, ExitLag, Overwolf, or Zer0  

## Enforcement

We may suspend or terminate access, invalidate sessions, and cooperate with providers or authorities where appropriate.

## Reporting

Report abuse or security issues through the Zer0 in-app Help Center (or the security channel designated by the Zer0 team).
