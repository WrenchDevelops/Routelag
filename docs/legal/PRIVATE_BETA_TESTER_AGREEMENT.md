## 1. Invitation-only access

You are invited as a private-beta tester. Access is personal and non-transferable. Do not share installers, invite codes, API endpoints, or credentials outside the approved tester group without written permission from the Zer0 team.

## 2. Confidentiality

Until Zer0 publicly announces general availability (or earlier written permission), you will not publicly post:

- Unpublished features, bugs, internal metrics, or capacity details  
- Screenshots/recordings that reveal unreleased UI if the team asks you not to  
- Server IPs, admin tooling, or security findings except via private report to the team  

Confidentiality lasts until Zer0 publicly announces general availability, and for **90 days thereafter**, unless information becomes public through no fault of yours.

Constructive private feedback to the Zer0 team is encouraged.

## 3. Software condition

You understand Zer0 is pre-release:

- Builds may be **unsigned**  
- Crashes, routing failures, and data loss are possible  
- Nodes may be taken offline for maintenance  
- Replay remains disabled while live PathGen is unsafe; do not assume replay is included  

## 4. Testing duties

When reasonably able, you agree to:

- Follow test instructions from the Zer0 team  
- Report severe networking issues promptly  
- Confirm whether **Restore Internet** recovered connectivity after failures  
- Avoid testing on critical production machines without a recovery plan
